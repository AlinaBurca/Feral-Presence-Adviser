Welcome to our help page for the web application dedicated to reporting stray animals! Here you will find essential information about how the application works and how you can use our platform to report stray animals or even report your lost pet.

How to report a pet

To report a stray or lost animal, please log in to your account or create a new account if you don't have one yet, then access our home page and select the corresponding option to add a new report. Fill in the requested information, such as the animal species, approximate location, and, if possible, attach a photo for easier identification. Additionally, you can add a description such as the animal's behavior or any other important aspect. If you spot a stray animal in your area, you can use our application to report the situation, thus contributing to increasing the chances of the animal being reunited with its owner or finding a new home.

How to use your account

After reporting an animal, you can access the dashboard section where you can view your submitted reports, track their status, and take further actions. Additionally, you have the option to update your account information, such as contact details or preferences, ensuring your profile remains up to date.

Features you need to know

Our application offers a range of features designed to enhance your experience and empower you to make a positive impact in your community. Clients can benefit from: access to comprehensive statistics related to the stray animal situation in their area, including trends over time, species distribution, and success rates of reunification or adoption efforts, the ability to customize and filter search results on the filtering page, enabling users to refine their search for lost or stray animals based on specific criteria such as species, location, name, ID and real-time notifications and updates on the status of reported animals, ensuring users stay informed about developments and outcomes. Also, by clicking on a card or announcement with an animal on the main page, you can access more detailed information about it.
