ABOUT FERAL PRESENCE ADVISER

Welcome to FePA!

Fepa is a dedicated service designed to streamline the process of finding lost and found pets by utilizing advanced communication technologies and an extensive network of community alerts. Fepa ensures that information about missing pets is quickly and efficiently spread throughout local areas. This innovative approach not only increases the chances of a timely reunion between pets and their owners but also mobilizes community members to participate actively in the search efforts. Our platform empowers pet owners and animal lovers by providing reliable tools and support to guide them through the recovery proces

Our missions are as follows:

To consistently create a regularly upgraded system to help boost the chances of getting to reunite lost and found pets back to their family.


To provide the best and most effective tools that can be useful in recovering lost & found pets, which will effectively provide actual results to the owners of the pets.
